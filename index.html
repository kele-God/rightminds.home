Our Rehabilitation Programs

Comprehensive, individualized care plans designed to address each person's unique needs and challenges.

Mental Health Rehabilitation

Specialized care for individuals with various mental health conditions including depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, schizophrenia, and PTSD.

  • Individual therapy sessions
  • Group therapy and peer support
  • Medication management
  • Life skills development
  • Family education and support

Addiction Recovery

Evidence-based treatments for substance use disorders, focusing on both physical healing and psychological well-being.

  • Medically supervised detoxification
  • Individual counseling
  • Group therapy and 12-step facilitation
  • Relapse prevention training
  • Aftercare planning and support

Dual Diagnosis Treatment

Integrated care for individuals experiencing both mental health issues and substance use disorders.

  • Coordinated treatment approach
  • Specialized dual diagnosis therapy
  • Comprehensive assessment
  • Personalized recovery planning
  • Long-term support strategies

Our Facilities

Creating healing environments that promote recovery, connection, and growth.

Residential Living

Our comfortable, home-like residences provide a safe and supportive environment for recovery. Each facility features:

  • Private and semi-private rooms
  • Common areas for socialization
  • Outdoor spaces and gardens
  • 24/7 staff support
  • Nutritious meal services

Therapeutic Spaces

Dedicated areas designed to facilitate healing and personal growth:

  • Individual therapy rooms
  • Group session spaces
  • Art and music therapy studios
  • Meditation and mindfulness areas
  • Physical activity spaces

Community Integration

Resources and spaces that help residents prepare for successful community reintegration:

  • Computer labs for education and job searching
  • Life skills training kitchen
  • Career development center
  • Community meeting rooms
  • Transportation services

Our Approach

A holistic methodology that addresses the whole person - mind, body, and spirit.

Person-Centered Care

We recognize that each individual has unique needs, strengths, and goals. Our treatment plans are tailored specifically to each person, with regular assessments and adjustments to ensure optimal progress.

Evidence-Based Practices

Our programs utilize therapeutic approaches that have been scientifically proven to be effective, including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, Dialectical Behavior Therapy, Motivational Interviewing, and trauma-informed care.

Holistic Wellness

We integrate complementary approaches such as nutrition counseling, physical fitness, mindfulness, stress management, and creative expression to support overall wellbeing and sustainable recovery.

Community Connection

We help residents build meaningful connections and develop support networks both within our program and in the broader community, which are essential for long-term recovery.
